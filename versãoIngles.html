About Us

Our goal as the development team of this project is to encourage awareness about racism and its intersections with other prejudices, mainly focusing on the individual who suffers discrimination, so they can understand when to defend themselves, but also provoking reflection in those who violate, intentionally or not.

The developers are first-year students of the IT course integrated with High School at IFSP Campus Caraguatatuba.

About the Project

Conscientiza is an educational initiative that seeks to promote dialogue on ethnic-racial relations, combating structural racism and valuing diversity. Through quality content, we seek to contribute to a more just and equal society.
