Inspiring Profiles

person

Djamila Ribeiro

Philosopher and writer

Black feminist philosopher and author of the best-seller “Small Anti-Racist Handbook”. A reference in debates on Black feminism and intersectionality.

person

Silvio Almeida

Jurist

Lawyer, philosopher and professor. Author of “Structural Racism”, a key work to understand the roots of racism in Brazilian society.

person

Lélia Gonzalez

Intellectual

Intellectual, professor and anthropologist, pioneer in race and gender studies in Brazil. Her ideas continue to influence generations.

person

Abdias do Nascimento

Activist

Activist, artist, writer and politician. Founder of the Black Experimental Theater and tireless defender of Black rights in Brazil.

About us

As the team developing this project, our aim is to encourage awareness about racism and its intersections with other forms of prejudice, focusing mainly on people who suffer discrimination, so they can recognize when and how to defend themselves, but also provoking reflection in those who commit violence, intentionally or not.

The developers are first-year students of the Integrated High School IT course at IFSP Campus Caraguatatuba.

About the project

Conscientiza is an educational initiative that promotes dialogue on ethnic-racial relations, structural racism and the value of diversity. Through quality content, we seek to contribute to a fairer and more equal society.
