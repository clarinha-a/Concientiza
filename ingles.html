our objective

Recognizing prejudice is the first step toward breaking the silence that sustains it. Often, it hides behind jokes, looks, or subtle exclusions, making it hard to identify. It’s important to reflect on one’s worth and understand that no one deserves to be treated as inferior. Awareness brings strength and builds self-esteem. Only by identifying injustice can we confront it and transform reality.

About us

As the team developing this project, our aim is to encourage awareness about racism and its intersections with other forms of prejudice, focusing mainly on people who suffer discrimination, so they can recognize when and how to defend themselves, but also provoking reflection in those who commit violence, intentionally or not.

The developers are first-year students of the Integrated High School IT course at IFSP Campus Caraguatatuba.

About the project

Conscientiza is an educational initiative that promotes dialogue on ethnic-racial relations, structural racism and the value of diversity. Through quality content, we seek to contribute to a fairer and more equal society.
